About Me:

Hi, I am Sethe, I like to think of myself as a caring mother but off late I seem to have failed at that. I made some of the toughest decisions a human should have to make but still I can't seem to catch a break. Growing up in isolation, I feel though that my own daughter may not have the societal development that she needs in order to escape the trauma that her previous generations have had to endure. I hope that I together with Paul D can piece together a semblance of life for her now that Beloved is gone. I still though am proud of everything she has done to this day and expect even greater things from her post college.

My thoughts on Beloved

Beloved represented something in me that I couldn't keep away from myself. I knew she was bad for me the moment she arrived but as a mother I felt a duty towards her care. As she evolved, I realized our roles had flipped and she started commanding me around. As a mother, you always do whats best for your kid so I obeyed unknowing what would become of me. After a point, I didn't know who I was and how much control over my own actions I had. Thankfully, my real daughter and husband saved me and for that I am incredibly grateful.
